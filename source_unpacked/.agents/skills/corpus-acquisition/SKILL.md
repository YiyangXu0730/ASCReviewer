# Corpus Acquisition


## Purpose

Plan and maintain a legally safe corpus for biomedical/cancer/bioinformatics writing review.

## Required outputs

- source policy
- acquisition plan
- source index
- license/reuse status
- metadata-only classification when reuse is unclear

## Candidate sources

Prefer PubMed/PMC/PMC OA, PLOS, BMC, Nature Portfolio OA journals, eLife, Bioinformatics, ICMJE, EQUATOR, PRISMA, STROBE, CONSORT where relevant. Always verify current source status and license before use.


## Evidence rules

- Teacher rubric rules have highest priority.
- Official guidelines and source-derived patterns support secondary evaluation.
- Domain expert heuristics must be labeled and cannot override teacher rules.
- If evidence is missing, ask for it.

## Stop conditions

Stop or mark provisional if:
- marking scheme is missing;
- source provenance is unclear;
- rule becomes too generic to guide review;
- task-specific requirement is being written into reusable core.
