### LibraryBuilder Changes

- Added official/open-access or metadata-only sources to `library/source_index.seed.json`.

  - FAIR principles for data stewardship.
  - TRIPOD and TRIPOD+AI for prediction-model reporting.
  - SAMPL for biomedical statistical reporting.
  - BRISQ for human biospecimen reporting.
  - Nature Portfolio, BMC Cancer, Cell Press STAR/KRT, and JCI author guidance for data/code/materials/resource reporting.
  - DESeq2 and cBioPortal method/pattern sources for bioinformatics and public cancer-genomics methods.
  - TCGA PRAD and advanced prostate cancer genomics papers as optional future specialization seed metadata only.

### AdversarialAuditor Objections

- Several high-quality sources are journal policies or guideline pages with reuse terms that vary; these must remain `metadata_only`.
- TRIPOD/TRIPOD+AI, ARRIVE, BRISQ, and SAMPL are study-design-specific. Applying them outside their design scope would be false precision.
- DESeq2 and cBioPortal are method/source-pattern anchors, not generic requirements to use a specific tool or portal.
- Prostate cancer sources risk topic hardcoding if connected to generic rules.
- PMCID/free availability is not equivalent to open reuse; do not store full text from `metadata_only` sources.

### Arbiter Decisions

- Accepted the source expansion.
- Marked journal policy pages, TRIPOD, SAMPL, BRISQ, cBioPortal, TCGA PRAD, and advanced prostate cancer sources as `metadata_only` where reuse was not clearly open.
- Marked PLOS/FAIR/DESeq2 entries as `open_reuse_verified` where source pages or metadata supported open access/CC reuse.
- Preserved prostate cancer papers only as optional domain seed metadata, not generic rule sources.
